# Welcome

This document presents a hybrid business model designed to deliver essential pet care services to vulnerable populations through a sustainable, impact-focused structure. The following pages contain detailed information for potential grantmakers, fiscal sponsors, and mission-aligned investors.

Here you will find:

- **The full business plan**: The operational framework, market analysis, and impact strategy.
- **The financial architecture**: A clear breakdown of our LLC + Fiscal Sponsor model and use of funds.
- **The investment thesis**: Why this scalable, person-centered approach addresses a critical community need.
- **This is not a public service website**. It is a proposal for partners who wish to invest in a replicable model of care that combines entrepreneurial efficiency with social purpose.
