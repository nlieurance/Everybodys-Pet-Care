# 2. Project description

- **Project Name**: Everybody’s Pet Care
- **Legal Structure**: Everybody’s Pet Care, LLC (A social enterprise operated by Nick Lieurance)
- **Fiscal Status**: Seeking a 501(c)(3) Fiscal Sponsor to receive grants and tax-deductible donations.
- **Mission Statement**: To strengthen our community by providing compassionate, accessible pet care to all, ensuring that every pet owner—regardless of income, age, ability, or their pet’s behavior—has the support they need to thrive together.
